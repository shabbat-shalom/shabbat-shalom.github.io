Halachic Disclaimer

The Shabbat and holiday times provided by this application are calculated based on standard astronomical algorithms and geographical coordinates. These times are intended for informational and planning purposes only.

Important: The precise observance of Shabbat and Jewish holidays may vary based on:

  • Local community customs (minhagim)
  • Specific rabbinic rulings
  • Geographical considerations
  • Individual family traditions

Candle lighting times are calculated 18 minutes before sunset by default, and Havdalah times are calculated 50 minutes after sunset. However, different communities may observe different customs (e.g., 40 minutes before sunset in Jerusalem, or 72 minutes after sunset for Havdalah in some communities).

For authoritative halachic times and guidance, please consult your local rabbi or rabbinic authority (posek).

About Jewish Times

Jewish Times (JTimes) is a modern, user-friendly Progressive Web App designed to help Jewish communities worldwide access accurate Shabbat and holiday times. Our mission is to make Jewish observance more accessible through technology while maintaining the highest respect for halachic standards and traditions.

This application is provided free of charge as a service to the Jewish community. If you find it useful, please consider supporting its continued development and maintenance.
